Reverse Engineering the Energy Pathway

Every muscle action consumes ATP — but where does that ATP come from? Trace backwards through the chain: the Sarcomere needs ATP, which is forged by ATP Synthase, which is driven by a proton gradient built by the electron transport chain, fuelled by carriers from the Krebs Cycle, which starts with pyruvate from glycolysis. Each module lets you interact with one stage, controlling the inputs and watching the outputs in real time.
